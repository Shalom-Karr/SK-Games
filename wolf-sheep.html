How to play

Wolf & Sheep (Volk i Ovtsy) is a folk chase game on the dark squares of a checkers board.

Setup

  • 4 sheep start on the dark squares of the back row.
  • 1 wolf starts on a dark square at the opposite end.

Moves

  • Sheep move 1 square diagonally, forward only, toward the wolf's start.
  • Wolf moves 1 square diagonally in any direction.
  • Pieces cannot share a square. No jumping, no captures.
  • Sheep move first, then alternate.

Winning

  • Sheep win if the wolf has no legal move.
  • Wolf wins if it reaches the sheep's starting row, or if every sheep is stuck.
🐑 Sheep tip

Move as one tight diagonal wall. Never let a sheep lag behind — a gap in the line is a hole the wolf walks through.

🐺 Wolf tip

Probe the edges. A sheep that breaks formation to chase you creates the hole you need.
